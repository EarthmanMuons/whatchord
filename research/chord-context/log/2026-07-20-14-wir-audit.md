# 2026-07-20: When-in-Rome realization audit passed

**Goal.** Discharge the protocol precondition on the When-in-Rome ruler (entry
2026-07-20-03): the realization and quality mapping must pass a stratified audit
with a recorded error rate before that ruler's first frozen result.

**Setup.** Second-interpreter audit
(`tool/chord-context/wir_realization_audit.py`): an independent realizer for the
common RomanText figure grammar, built on the DCML extractor's line-of-fifths
arithmetic and deliberately not on music21, re-derives each scored event's
expected root and quality from figure plus local key. Agreement is stratified by
figure family; anything outside the grammar is enumerated as the manual-review
residue. The audit tool's own first pass misread three minor-mode dialect rules
(lowercase vi/vii take the raised melodic degree, uppercase VII the subtonic; i7
keeps the natural seventh) and was corrected to the dialect the corpus's
analysts write, which is itself evidence that the music21 path implements that
dialect.

```sh
python3 tool/chord-context/wir_realization_audit.py \
  --labels build/chord-context/labels/when-in-rome-v1.labels.json
```

**What happened.** 3,848 scored events audited: 3,846 agree (99.95%). The two
residual disagreements are interpreter-dialect corners, not label errors: `bvi`
in E minor (whether the flat is redundant with the natural submediant; music21
reads C minor, matching analyst intent) and `V65/vi` in E minor (the applied
target `vi` on the raised submediant; music21 G#7, again the intent). Mechanized
label-error count: zero.

Manual residue: 61 events across 27 distinct figures, all explained by
inspection: 5-4 suspensions and cadential six-fours (`V54` 16, `Cad64` 12),
ninth figures the grammar skips (`V9` family 11, realized in the labels as
superset mappings), nonstandard figured bass (`viio63` 5), the ASCII `/o`
half-diminished spelling (5), a triad-labeled `viiø6` (2), and single-instance
analyst shorthands (`I6+`, `i5`, `Ib7`, `ib3`, `v5`, `iv6b5`, `Imaj7`). None is
a realization error; the suspension figures are acceptable-answer-set material
and sit outside the scored pools when their sounding sets mismatch.

**Plain-English reading.** Two independent programs, sharing no code and no
interpreter, now derive the same expected chord for 99.95% of scored
When-in-Rome events, and every remaining difference is a documented dialect
question where the music21 reading is what the analyst meant. The label pipeline
the scouting studies used is validated; the corrections made along the way were
to the auditor, not the labels.

**Decisions.**

- The When-in-Rome audit precondition is discharged with a recorded mechanized
  error rate of zero label errors (99.95% second-interpreter agreement, residue
  enumerated and inspected).
- Acceptable-answer sets remain built only when contestable categories are
  scored; they are excluded from the scored pools today, so When-in-Rome
  clean-pool results are frozen-grade from this entry forward. Entry
  2026-07-19-01 remains scouting historically; the ruler's numbers as
  re-measured since (the lever 0 A/B arms and after) carry full status.
- The history-relabel front was reviewed separately the same day and stays open
  pending data (entry -13).
